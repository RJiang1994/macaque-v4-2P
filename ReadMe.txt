4x
The codes for 4x imaging in FDR_Permutation_4x.m
The mean df/f0 map and the variance map can be found in Data_4x_A.mat and Data_4x_B.mat. The df/f0 map for each single trial can be found in https://osf.io/xmj5c/

16x
The codes for 4x imaging in ResultPlot_16x.m
Cell ROI pixels are given in CCA (monkeyA) and CCB (monkeyB).
Stimuli are given in StimContour and StimGrating.
Responses are in the Rsp matrices. The dimensions are CellNum x RepeatNum x StimNum.
